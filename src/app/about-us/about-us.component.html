Our Mission
Our mission is to empower small and medium farmers by bringing technology, machinery and quality agri services into hands of farmers.
One Stop Destination
KharaAgrotech is building the much needed basic supply chain infrastructure for farmers to bring technology, machinery and agri services which are already buit or out there into the hands of farmers in States like Bihar, UP and Jharkhand. GreenSupply is one stop destination for all agri service like quality agri inputs, technology, equipments, advisory and market linkage under one roof at farmer's village. We partner with agri business, organizations and institutions to make all agri services accessible and offordable to every farmers.
Growth & Evolution
KharaAgrotech is evolve after working at grassroot level with farmers to solve their pain (facing with traditional system and farmers dealings with multiple people in process with production and selling). Currently, we are working with 20000+ farmers in Patna, Bihar to support they need and providing all encompassing solution to increase their income.
